About Me

You see "Emissions Trading" and "Energy-Climate Policy Modelling" on my profile, and yeah, that's a big part of what I do. But what that really means is I'm constantly digging into data, building models (lots of love for MESSAGEix!), and trying to make sense of how we can transition to a cleaner energy future.

It's not just about the numbers, though. It's about understanding the real-world implications and finding pathways that are both effective and fair.
